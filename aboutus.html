Who We Are

Welcome to Zanam Foundation

Established in 2021

The organization was conceived with the idea of facilitating a process of development for the vulnerable sections of the society.

The underlying philosophy behind the primary focus of the organization lies in strengthening child protection systems and working on disability issues. Besides this, we also have programs dedicated to Child Welfare, Fighting Substance Abuse, Basic Health Camps, Promoting Reading Rooms, Humanitarian Aid and Women Empowerment.

We envision a society where caring families, alert communities and responsive governments come together to ensure the rights and protection of all marginalized people.

We are an organization committed to empowering children through Advocacy, Campaigning and Sensitization, besides raising awareness about the rights of children and the differently abled in society. It has always been our endeavor to lend our support to the marginalized in creating the right environments they need in order to thrive and enhance their lives.

The major part of our work in the past 11 years has been child centric and particularly so, because we genuinely believe that children have a memory to carry forward and therefore a secure childhood would ensure that they become happy adults of tomorrow. If we want to bring about change, we need to invest in children and young people.

Our aim is to advocate, lobby and network with government and non-government organizations and institutes for addressing common issues of concern.
